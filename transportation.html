2023 Fall NABC Transportation Guide

Traveling to Atlanta is probably the easiest city in the world to get to. The Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International airport is the busiest and most efficient airport in the world and, by some accounts, the best in North America.

Hartsfield-Jackson is a global gateway, offering nonstop service from more than 150 domestic and 70 international destinations. These locales include major commercial centers in Europe, Asia, the Caribbean, Africa, and South and Central America.

Once in Atlanta, MARTA, Atlanta’s rail system, is the quickest and most cost-effective way to get to the Atlanta Marriott Marquis hotel. The airport MARTA station is located in the ground transportation area, near baggage claim. Exit the train at the Peachtree Center MARTA Station. Once at the station, follow the signs to the Harris Street exit after getting off the train, and then to Peachtree Street East. At street level, turn right to walk north along Peachtree Street. Turn right again at the first intersection on Harris Street, then left at the next block on Peachtree Center Avenue. Approximate walking time from the fare gate is only three minutes. If you don’t want to walk outside, you can also exit the station into the Peachtree Center mall where there’s a skybridge connection.

Trains run every 10 minutes on weekdays and every 20 minutes on weekends. MARTA fare is $2.50.

Alternatively, Hartsfield-Jackson's Rental Car Center (RCC) houses 12 rental car brands. Operating 24 hours a day, seven days a week, the RCC is just a few minutes away from ATL via the SkyTrain, an electric-powered automated people mover system.
